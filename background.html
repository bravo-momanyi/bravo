My Background

My name is Bravin Momanyi Osoro, also known as Bravo. I was born on November 22, 2006, in a peaceful and beautiful village called Kiomakondo, located in the Mwamosioma area of Kisii County, Kenya.

I was raised and still live in Kiomakondo. The quiet environment, green hills, and strong community spirit shaped my values: simplicity, humility, and hard work. Life in the village made me curious, creative, and focused.

Since childhood, I’ve had a deep interest in understanding how things work — especially in technology. That curiosity led me into the world of computers, design, and development. I enjoy creating systems and solutions that make life easier and more efficient.

Today, I am more than a student. I am a system builder in the making — with a dream to transform lives through software and creativity, starting right from my small village to the world.
